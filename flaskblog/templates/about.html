MediSphere

A web application to diagnose the disease based on the symptoms given by the user and suggesting the preventions to deal with some common disease.

Why diagnose disease?

Disease diagnosis plays a pivotal role in healthcare informatics. It is crucial to diagnose the disease at an early stage. The lack or delay in proper treatment can also lead to the death of patients. Therefore disease diagnosis can prove to be life saving in many cases. Disease diagnose system can assist doctors, physicians and healthcare professionals in effective decision making for disease diagnosis as well.

Features

  1. Disease Diagnosis

    The disease is diagnosed using the algorithm(Decision Tree Classifier) and the user has to enter the symptoms from the given drop-down menu.

  2. Health Manoeuvre

    User is provided with resources for preventing common diseases. Insighful information related to yoga asanas are given for preventing diseases.

  3. Bulletin & MediBot

    The web application provides latest health related news from all over the world and provide location of nearby health centers. Have conversation with our medibot.
